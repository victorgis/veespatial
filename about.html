Who am I?

Hi, I am Victor (Veespatial), a GIS Consultant living in the beautiful city of Uyo, Nigeria. I'm passionate about using Geographic Information System (GIS) technology in solving real-world problems. I have over 6 years experience in providing project and research support to drive success via Geographic Information System.

I currently work with an amazing team at Releaf Marketplace Limited, as a Lead GIS Developer. At Releaf, I demonstrate my GIS capabilities using ArcGIS JavaScript API, along with VueJS, to build and manage a web app that allows you to position factories in their best possible and suitable location, given the particular focus on environmental, cultural, and socio-economic factors.

In 2020, I was opportune to build the Akwa Ibom State Covid-19 ArcGIS Operations Dashboard, during the covid-19 surge. The dashboard was developed using ArcGIS online platform under Africa-Geoportal licence. The dashboard was used to monitor and enhance case-finding efforts as well as operations management and decision-making as regards emergency response for the state.

Also, I have hosted several virtual humanitarian mapping exercises. Last year, in June 2021, I co-hosted one for the improvement of HIV/AIDS & Gender-based violence (GBV) projects in Ibeno LGA, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. You can read up about it here: https://precisegis.com.ng/humanitarian-mapping-exercise-for-improvement-in-hiv-aids-gender-based-violence-gbv-projects-in-nigeria/

Furthermore, I've been a Data & GIS Analyst with FHI 360, in Akwa Ibom State under the SIDHAS project in collaboration with the Ministry of Health as a MERL intern.

I am a member of the Geospatial Forum of Nigeria (GNF), I have been directly involved in conducting & facilitating various trainings/workshops for both GIS and Data Science specialists across Nigeria on the GNP platform.

Furthermore, I'm skilled in the use of Leaflet, Geoserver, PostGIS, GeoDjango, QGIS, ArcGIS, Python Scripting & Remote Sensing.

If you agree with me that I'm a great choice, kindly contact me via my contact channels.

Thank you.
